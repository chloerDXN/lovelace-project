Soy Patties

  Vegetable




More Vegetable Recipes
Ingredients:

  • 2 cups soybean pulp
  • 2 cups natural brown rice(cooked)
  • 2 Tbsp. vegetable fat
  • 1 onion, chopped fine
  • 1 Tbsp. soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • flavor with garlic, sage or whole wheat bread crumbs

Directions:
Mix the first seven ingredients thoroughly together,and shape into patties. Roll the patties in whole wheat breadcrumbs. Bake in a greased pan until brown, or warm in a frying pan, but do not fry.(I have never tried this recipe,so I hope it turns out okay)
