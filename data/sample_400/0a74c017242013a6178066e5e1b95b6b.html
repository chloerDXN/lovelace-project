White Chocolate-Cream Cheese Frosting

(25)

Melted white chocolate, cream cheese, butter and powdered sugar are beaten into a fluffy frosting to spread over cakes and cupcakes.

What You Need

1
pkg.  (8 oz.) PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
1/4
cup  (1/2 stick) butter or margarine, softened
1
pkg.  (6 squares) BAKER'S White Chocolate, melted, cooled slightly
1
tsp.  vanilla
2
cups  powdered sugar

Make It

BEAT cream cheese and butter in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until well blended. Add melted chocolate and vanilla; mix well.

ADD sugar gradually, beating until light and fluffy after each addition.

USE to frost your favorite cake or cupcake recipe.

Kraft Kitchens Tips

Note
Recipe makes enough to frost tops and sides of 2 (8- or 9-inch) cake layers, top and sides of 13x9-inch cake, tops of 3 (8- or 9-inch) cake layers or 24 cupcakes.
Special Extra
For a perfectly smooth frosting, measure the powdered sugar, then sift it with a hand sifter or mesh strainer before adding it to the cream cheese mixture.
