Southern Comfort: 15 Pecan Pie Recipes

Whether it's the traditional Southern go-to recipe or a version laced with chocolate, bourbon, or other indulgent additions, pecan pie is that irresistibly sweet dessert everyone loves to eat around Thanksgiving, Kentucky Derby day, or at any other time of year. Its sweet, gooey crunch is the perfect way to end a savory meal. Try the classic Southern recipe here, or experiment with one of the other addictive pecan pie variations.
