Ingredients

Servings          
Original recipe makes 1
3 tbolive oil
1/2 cWine
Pasta
2 lbfresh tomatoes; Very ripe
1/3 cFresh basil; chopped
2 Clovesgarlic; minced
Black pepper
1/2 cHeavy cream
1/2 tsSalt

Pasta with Tomato Basil Cream Sauce Preparation

Heat oil in skillet. Saute garlic in oil for 30 seconds, stirring constantly. Remove skillet from heat. In a food processor or blender, combine peeled tomatoes, salt and chopped basil. Process until combined, but still a little chunky. Return skillet to heat. Pour in tomato mixture and add wine. Simmer over low heat for 7 to 8 minutes. Season to taste with pepper. Stir in cream and heat (over low) until warm throughout. Serve over your favorite pasta, prepared according to package directions.
