Boiled Salad Dressing

2 reviews

  • Review
  • Print recipe
  • Email recipe

Ingredients

1 egg
3 tablespoons sugar
3 tablespoons vinegar
3 tablespoons water
1 teaspoon mustard
1 teaspoon butter

Method

  1. Beat together egg and sugar
  2. Add vinegar, water, mustard and butter
  3. Cook until boiling.

Recipe notes

This is one of my very favourite old recipe's for a salad dressing. Try it I am sure you will love it.
