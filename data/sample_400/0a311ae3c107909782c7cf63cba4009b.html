7-Day Dinner Planner

Our menu helps you plan out dinner and your shopping list for the week.

Monday -- Taco Night

Let the slow cooker do all the work for you with this easy recipe. Set up bowls of toppings so everyone can build their own tacos.
  • Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos
  • Easy Fiesta Beans
  • Frozen Horchata
  • See More Slow Cooker Recipes

Tuesday -- Easy, Cheesy Casserole

You can't beat the prep time of this hearty dinner. The simple flavor even pleases picky eaters.
  • Almost Lasagna
  • Broccolini with Garlic
  • Mini Whoopie Pies
  • See More Easy Casseroles

Wednesday -- Quick-Fix Chicken

Get creative with boneless chicken breasts. This fast, delicious recipe is easier and cheaper than ordering take-out.
  • Easy Chicken Lo Mein
  • No-Bake Cookie Squares
  • See More Quick Fixes for Chicken Breasts

Thursday -- Totally Turkey

It's turkey time in no time with this quick and easy recipe. Use different colored peppers for a fun presentation the kids will love.
  • Stuffed Bell Peppers
  • Roasted Zucchini and Pesto
  • Chewy Chocolate Cream Sandwich Cookies
  • See More Quick Turkey Recipes

Friday -- Meat-Free Meal

Whether you're a vegetarian for life or just for the night, you'll love this satisfying, savory bread pudding.
  • Asparagus Bread Pudding
  • Carrot Salad
  • Apple and Berry Crumbles
  • See More Meatless Meals

Saturday -- Pizza Party

Throw a little pizza party with these fun and easy recipes for kid-friendly pies.
  • Mini Muffin Pizzas
  • Ham and Cheese Pit-zas
  • Fairy Cupcakes
  • See More Kid-Friendly Dinners

Sunday -- Big Family Dinner

This classic roast chicken dinner is packed with hearty root vegetables. Serve with creamy buttermilk mashed potatoes, and finish off with fresh-baked chocolate chip cookies.
  • Roast Chicken and Roots
  • Buttermilk Mashed Potatoes
  • Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies
  • See More Classic Family Meals
