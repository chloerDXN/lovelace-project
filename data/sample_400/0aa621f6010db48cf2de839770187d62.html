Carolina Baked Dressing

Recipe At A Glance


Ready in: 1-2 hrs ?


Serves/Makes:   25

  



INGREDIENTS:

6 cups crumbled cornbread
5 cups crumbled biscuits
3/4 stick butter
1 cup chopped onion
4 eggs, slightly beaten
1 cup chopped celery
7 cups chicken or turkey stock
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons sage


DIRECTIONS:

Mix all ingredients well.

Bake in pans in a preheated 350 oven for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
