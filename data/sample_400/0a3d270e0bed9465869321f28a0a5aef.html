Easy Creamy Chicken Pasta

Recipe #2706

Submitted by: Fairlie

Serves: 4

Preparation time: 15 to 30 minutes

22 reviews

  • Serves: 4
  • Preparation time: 15 to 30 minutes
  • Quick to make and a good way to use up left over roast chicken.
  • Review
  • Print recipe
  • Email recipe
  • Add to Recipe Book

Ingredients

1 breast roast chicken, shredded
1 small onion, chopped
1 teaspoon crushed garlic
2 tablespoons Moroccan or chicken seasoning
300 g pasta of choice
250 mL cream
Salt and pepper, to taste
Grated parmesan cheese

Method

  1. Cook pasta.
  2. In a large frypan lightly saute onion and garlic in a little butter or oil.
  3. Add shredded chicken and sprinkle over Moroccan seasoning. Mix well.
  4. Pour in cream and bring to boil, reduce temperature and simmer gently.
  5. The sauce will reduce and thicken.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to your liking
  7. Once pasta is cooked, drain and add to sauce, mix well.
  8. Serve and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.

Recipe notes

Fantastic with a bit of garlic bread on the side. Bacon can be used instead of chicken. You can use any part of the chicken. Light cream or evaporated milk can be used to lower fat content.
