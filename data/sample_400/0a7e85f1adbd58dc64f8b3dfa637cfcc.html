TIPS & ADVICE

Plank Grilling

Fish is the original favorite, but grilling with wood planks introduces a whole new range of savory flavors to veggies, meats and more.

Smoking Foods

Smoking is a "low and slow" method of indirect heat cooking. If you don't own a smoker, don't let that stop youyou can use your regular charcoal grill to prepare incredibly tender, mouthwatering smoked foods.

Marinades, Brines and Rubs

Salt it, soak it, rub it down. Sometimes meats need a little extra love before they're ready for the grill.
